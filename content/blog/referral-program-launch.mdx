The Echobell referral program is live. Share your invite code, earn points, and redeem them for free Premium.

### How points work

- **You earn** 20 points each time a friend subscribes to or renews a [Monthly Plan](/en#pricing), and 200 points for an [Annual Plan](/en#pricing).
- **Your friend earns** 100 points for using your invite code within their first 30 days.

### Where to find your code

Go to **Settings → Invite Friends** in the [Echobell app](https://apps.apple.com/app/apple-store/id6743597198?pt=128151925&ct=blog-referral-program-launch-en&mt=8) to generate your code or enter someone else's.

### Redeeming points

- **200 points** = 1 month of Premium
- **2000 points** = 1 year of Premium

### Requirements

Update to Echobell **version 1.22** or higher before you start.

### Already a user?

Existing users can enter an invite code before **January 15, 2026** to claim the 100-point welcome bonus.
