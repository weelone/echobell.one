# Integrate AiCoin

[AiCoin](https://www.aicoin.com) is a professional cryptocurrency market data and trading tool. By integrating with Echobell, you can receive AiCoin's price warnings and indicator signals as instant phone calls or notifications, ensuring you never miss a trading opportunity.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ## Create a Channel in Echobell

    1. Open the Echobell app.
    2. Tap the **+** button to create a new channel.
    3. Name it "AiCoin Alerts" or something similar.
    4. Choose an icon and color that you like.
    5. In the channel details, copy the **Webhook URL**.

  </Step>

  <Step>
    ## Configure AiCoin Alert

    1. Open the AiCoin PC Client.
    2. Navigate to the K-line chart of the trading pair you want to monitor.
    3. Click on **Alert** (or the bell icon) and select **Add Alert**.
    4. Set your trigger conditions (e.g., Price > 60000).
    5. In the notification settings, check the **Webhook** option.
    6. Paste your Echobell Webhook URL into the field.

  </Step>

  <Step>
    ## Test the Integration

    Save the alert. When the condition is met, AiCoin will send a request to Echobell, and you will receive an instant notification or call on your device.

  </Step>
</Steps>
